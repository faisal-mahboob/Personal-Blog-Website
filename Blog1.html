Istanbul, Turkey

Thosuands of years of history meshed in an amazing location.


A few reasons to visit Turkey:

  • Tourish is a big part of the Turkish Economy. They have hundreds of inexpensive hotels set up all over the town.
  • You'll find too many bazaars with fresh organic food and cultural colorful items passed down the generation.
  • Turkish food is always very delicious and has a huge variety that might require multiple trips to Turkey to sample them all completely.

Indonesia

It is so green, you'll feel like it might be heaven :).

A few reasons to visit Indonesia:

  • It is a country of multiple islands. They have a very long list of naturally beautiful spots that you might love to camp in with friends.
  • Prices are often quite low in Indonesia , so you can find a place to stay for cheap.
  • Food is lovely and very unique for south east asian people. You will love to immerse yourself in their culture and get a break from working life.

Morocco

It's warm and friendly :).

A few reasons to visit Morocco:

  • They have very nice architecture spread around the country.
  • It is warm and easy to walk around all day and everyday. Prices of both food and hotels are very cheap.
  • People are friendly and you will get to experience their culture which is unique and colorful.
